Risk Assessment & Mitigation

🔴 High Priority: AI Model Performance

Risk that machine learning models don't achieve target accuracy levels, impacting core value proposition.

Mitigation: Parallel development of multiple model approaches, extensive testing with real customer data, and fallback to rule-based systems.

🔴 High Priority: Integration Complexity

Third-party API integrations may be more complex than anticipated, causing delays in core functionality.

Mitigation: Early API testing, dedicated integration team, and phased rollout starting with most critical platforms.

🟡 Medium Priority: Team Capacity

Key team members may become unavailable due to competing priorities or personal circumstances.

Mitigation: Cross-training team members, detailed documentation, and identified backup resources.

🟡 Medium Priority: Market Competition

Competitors may launch similar products before our go-to-market date, reducing our competitive advantage.

Mitigation: Continuous competitive monitoring, unique differentiators, and accelerated launch timeline where possible.

🟢 Low Priority: Budget Overrun

Project costs may exceed allocated budget due to scope creep or unexpected expenses.

Mitigation: Weekly budget reviews, strict change management process, and 15% contingency buffer.
